About AnagramGen

Free, fast, and privacy-friendly text tools for everyone.

Our mission is to provide simple, free, browser-based text tools that work instantly — no sign-up, no tracking, no ads blocking your way.

AnagramGen started as a simple anagram finder and grew into a suite of everyday text tools. All tools run entirely in your browser — your text is never sent to any server.
